Early Life & Background

Lee Jin-wook was born in Cheongju, North Chungcheong Province, as the youngest son of a civil servant father. His family lived modestly, and he was a very quiet, observant, and thoughtful child who never participated in school talent shows. Everyone was surprised when he chose acting, given his reserved personality.

He enrolled in Environmental Engineering at Cheongju University but dropped out at age 21 to pursue his dream in Seoul. Despite family opposition, he moved to the capital, lived with his sister or friends, and worked part-time jobs (construction sites, cafes) to support himself. He saved money to attend acting classes, created his own portfolio, and distributed it to agencies and magazines. In 2003 he debuted as a print model in a Panasonic commercial and quickly landed 9 commercials within one year.

He belongs to the Jeongju Lee clan and is a descendant of King Sejong the Great through Prince Milseong (12th son). He often jokes about having “royal blood” and says it gives him a sense of elegance and responsibility in acting roles (e.g., in The Three Musketeers).

Career Overview

Debuted in acting with small roles in 2005 (Resurrection). Gained recognition in 2006 with Alone in Love, Smile Again, and Someday. In 2007 he played the lead in Air City opposite Choi Ji-woo (they dated from 2009–2011).

Completed mandatory military service (May 2009 – March 2011) at the Defense Media Agency. Post-discharge comeback: Spy Myung-wol (2011), I Need Romance 2012 (2012), and breakthrough hit Nine (2013 – time-travel romance that created massive fandom).

Major films: Miss Granny (2014 – supporting), The Target (2014 – first lead film role), The Beauty Inside (2015 – iconic theater cheer scene), Time Renegades (2016), A Tiger in Winter (2017), High Society (2018), A Year-End Medley (2021), Dark Nuns (2025 – Father Paolo).

Key dramas: The Time We Were Not in Love (2015), Goodbye Mr. Black (2016), Return (2018), Voice S2-S3 (2018–2019 – OCN record ratings), Sweet Home S1-S3 (2020–2024 – Netflix global phenomenon), Bulgasal: Immortal Souls (2021–2022), Welcome to Wedding Hell (2022), Doona! (2023 cameo), Squid Game S2-S3 (2024–2025 – Player 246), Dear Hyeri (2024), Beyond the Bar (2025 – lawyer Yoon Seok-hoon), Seven O'Clock Breakfast Club for the Brokenhearted (2025–2026).

Upcoming 2026: TYGO (Netflix action film with Don Lee & BLACKPINK Lisa), Wed Thurs Fri (romance drama opposite Shin Min-ah – confirmed January 2026).

Recent highlights: 2025 LACMA Art+Film Gala appearance (new bearded, bulkier look – preparing for new roles). January 2026 – official model for Jeong Kwan-jang Chunnok brand.

Personal Life

Dated actress Choi Ji-woo (2009–2011, met on Air City; breakup due to intense media pressure). Dated Gong Hyo-jin (May–September 2014, 3 months).

In July 2016 he was accused of sexual assault. After investigation he was fully cleared. The accuser was convicted of defamation and sentenced to 8 months suspended prison term (February 2018 appeal verdict).

As of 2026: Single. He keeps private life very low-key. Views on love: “Before marriage nothing is permanent. I don’t want to be just a boyfriend – I want to be a good man.” Perfectionist, responsible, cautious in relationships.
